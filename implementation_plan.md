# Implementation Plan - Lazlle Workspace Redesign

This document outlines the changes made to the Lazlle & Co website to align with the new "Product Studio" positioning, focusing on outcomes, AI, and automation.

## 1. Hero Section (`components/homepage/HeroSection.tsx`)
- **Headline**: Changed to "The Operating System for Modern Companies."
- **Subheadline**: Emphasized "digital product studio", "intelligent SaaS", "AI agents", and "automated workflows".
- **Trust Text**: "Trusted by forward-thinking startups and enterprises".
- **CTA**: "Start Your Project".

## 2. Services Overview (`components/homepage/Services.tsx`)
- **Structure**: Repurposed fit the 4 Service Pillars.
- **Pillars**:
  1. **Web & Product Experiences**: Conversion marketing sites, SaaS dashboards.
  2. **AI Agents & Smart Systems**: Calling/Chat agents, copilots.
  3. **Business Process Automation**: Workflow design, removing data silos.
  4. **Custom SaaS & Internal Tools**: Multi-tenant platforms, security.
- **Section Header**: "Comprehensive solutions for modern growth."

## 3. Capabilities / Products (`components/homepage/WhyChooseUs.tsx`)
- **Structure**: Renamed section to "Capabilities" (Outcomes).
- **Outcome Blocks**:
  1. **Next-Gen Websites**: Capture & convert leads.
  2. **24/7 AI Agents**: Qualify leads, book meetings.
  3. **Unified Dashboards**: Total visibility.
  4. **Zero-Touch Workflows**: Eliminate manual entry.
  5. **Scalable Architecture**: Growth-ready systems.
  6. **Actionable Intelligence**: Data-driven insights.

## 4. How We Work (`components/homepage/HowWeWork.tsx`)
- **Process Steps**: Updated to 5-step flow.
  1. **Discover**: Audit & Analysis.
  2. **Design**: Blueprint & Strategy.
  3. **Build**: Development & Integration.
  4. **Automate**: Launch & Training.
  5. **Optimize**: Support & Scaling.

## 5. Founder & Vision / Social Proof (`components/homepage/WhoWeAre.tsx`)
- **Headline/Desc**: Introduced Harsh's vision of democratizing enterprise-grade automation.
- **Social Proof**: Added "outcome snippets" to the stats section (60% less manual time, 100% data accuracy, 3x faster response).

## 6. Final CTA (`components/homepage/Faq.tsx` & `components/homepage/Footer.tsx`)
- **FAQ Section**: Updated bottom CTA to "Start your automation journey".
- **Footer**: Kept existing layout but ensured alignment with new tone.

## 7. Page Layout (`app/page.tsx`)
- **Reordered Sections**: Hero -> Services -> Capabilities -> HowWeWork -> Founder/Vision -> FAQ -> Footer.

All changes adhere to the minimal, confident, and product-first tone requested.
